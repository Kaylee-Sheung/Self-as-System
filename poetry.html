✧ Therapy ✧

✧

In 2020,

The world starts to fall apart.

✧

Cyberbully, depression, anxiety disorder,

Insomnia, neurasthenia, corona-virus,

Disaster, chaos, disease...

People have to find a therapy method.

✧

Study, literature, communication,

Readings, books, articles,

Astronomy, physics, psychology...

Thereare lots of solutions, but nothing works.

✧

Html, css, javascript,

Coding, engineering, designing,

Technology, developments, production...

There's no way to find a correct answer for future.

✧

And god said,

The only antidote extists among human;

Above the high mountains,

Under the deep sea;

It is the most appreciating thing ever.

✧

It is,

LOVE.
